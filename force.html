Force And Laws Of Motion

  • laws of motions are:-
    1. A body which is in the rest or motion will rest in its state and change only when a external force is applied to it.
    2. The rate of change of momentum on an object is directly proportional to force applied on it.
    3. Every action has equal and opposite reactions.
  • Force(F):-The product of mass and accleration is known as force.
  • Momentum(M):-The product of mass and velocity is known as momentum.

    • F=ma
