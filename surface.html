Surface Area And V olumes

Surface area and volumes is a chapter of mathematics which deals with area and volumes of 3-D and 2-D figures.It is also related with surface area of cube,cuboid,circle,sphere,and other shapes.

some formulas related to this chapter are:-

  • LSA of cube=4a2
  • TSA of cube=6a2
  • LSA of cuboid=2(l+b)h
  • TSA of cuboid=2(lb+bh+hl)
  • area of circle=pi*r2
  • circumfrence=2*pi*r
  • TSA of sphere=4*pi*r2
  • volume of cone=4/3*pi*r3
