Gravitation

Some definitions related to this chapter are:-

  1. Law of gravitation:-Every object in the universe is exerting force on each other and the the force exerted is directly proportional to masses of the object and inversely proportional to the distance between them.
  2. Gravitational accleration:-Every heavenly object exerts a force on the object which is thrown upwards.Due to force exerted,it acclerate towards the planets, this value is called Gravitational accleration.It is denoted by 'g'.The value of 'g' on the earth is 9.8m/sec2.
