Arithmetic Progeressions

A sequence in which each term differs from its preceding term by a constant is called an Arithmetic progression. It is written as AP. The constant is called common difference of the an AP.

Some formulas related to APs are:-

  • an=a+(n-1)d
  • Sn=n(2a+(n-1)d/2
  • Sn=n(a+l)/2
  • d=an-an-1
  • an=Tn-Tn-1
  • 3 consecutive APs=(a-d),a,(a+d)
