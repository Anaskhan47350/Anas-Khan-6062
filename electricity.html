E lectricity

Electricity is a chapter of physics which is related with charge,curent and potential difference in a wire.

Some definitions related to this chapters are:-

  1. Charge:-It is a physical property of a quanta of matter.It is of two types
    • positive charge:-The particles of matter which contain more number of proton than electron are positively charge.
    • Negative charge:-The particles which contain more number of electron than a proton are negatively charge.
  2. Current:-Curent is movement of charge particles in a conductor when a potential difference is applied to its ends.Its S.I unit is ampere.It is denoted by I
  3. voltage:-It is energy to move a charge particles from infinity to a point.It is also called potential difference.Its S.I unit is volt(V).
