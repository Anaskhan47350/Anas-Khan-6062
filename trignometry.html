Trignometric Identities

Trignometry is a branch of mathematics which corporate with angle-side relation in a right-angled triangle.
It consists of some identities which are given below:-

  • cos2A+sin2A=1
  • 1+tan2A=sec2A
  • cot2A+1=cosec2A
