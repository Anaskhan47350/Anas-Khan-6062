Motion

In the motion we will study about the speed, distance,equations of motion's etc.

  1. Speed:-It is defined as rate of change of distance in respect of a reference point.Its S.I unit is m/sec. S=D/T
  2. Distance:-It defines how much a body has travelled.Its S.I unit is metre(m). D=ST
  3. displacement:-Distance of straight line between starting and final point is known as displacement.It is a vector quantity.
  4. Velocity:-Rate of change of displacement is known as Velocity.
  5. Some formulas related to this chapter are:-
    • S=D/T
    • V=Displacement/Time
    • v=u+at
    • S=ut+at2/2
    • v2=u2+2as
